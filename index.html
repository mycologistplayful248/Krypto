Krypto: The Superdog

Superman's Loyal Companion

About Krypto

Krypto the Superdog is more than just a pet — he’s Superman’s best friend and a hero in his own right.

Originally from Krypton, Krypto was sent to Earth by Superman’s father, Jor-El, as a test subject before baby Kal-El’s rocket was launched. Due to a navigation error, Krypto's ship arrived much later — but he eventually found his way to young Clark Kent on Earth.

Just like Superman, Krypto gained powers from Earth’s yellow sun. His abilities include:

  • Flight
  • Super strength
  • Heat vision
  • Super hearing
  • Super bark

Krypto is fiercely loyal and often joins Superman to protect Earth. He also stars in his own adventures and has teamed up with the Legion of Super-Pets.

With movie appearances and a comic crossover with Marvel’s Jeff the Land Shark, Krypto is getting the spotlight he truly deserves.
